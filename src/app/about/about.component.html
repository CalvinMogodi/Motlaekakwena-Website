Let Us Tell You Our Story


Motlaekakwena (Pty) Ltd Close Corporation was established in March 2015 by the founder members and it has grown from small beginnings to what the company represents today. The creation or formation of the company was to provide the corporate market with quality services and quality products and turn the key solutions through the enabling of all other activities.

Motlaekakwena (Pty) Ltd works as a centralized, professional, and efficient team, whose vision is to become a premier South African reseller of world-class products and business services. The company is a small to medium sized enterprise business, bridging the gap between main dealers of products and services, and the corporate client.
